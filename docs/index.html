About the Project

LaPlace is an open source app that brings different artificial intelligence models together in one space. Unlike cloud-based solutions, here you choose what to download, control how to use it, and adapt the app to your workflow. Everything runs locally - fast and with complete control in your hands.

The key difference is freedom: you can use anything from lightweight models for everyday tasks to more robust options, create your own workspace within the app, and integrate specific workflows like pentesting, development, or creative automation. Simple, practical, and powerful.
